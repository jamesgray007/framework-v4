# Microsoft — Knowledge File

## Positioning

Microsoft is consolidating its AI strategy around three throughlines: (1) shifting per-user businesses to "per-user + usage" pricing, (2) building Agent 365 as the central control plane for enterprise AI agents, and (3) treating coding/productivity/security as the structural verticals AI plugs into. Nadella's public framing: "agents are the new apps."

## Product Moves

- **2026-05-11** — Microsoft 365 E7 ("Frontier Suite") reached general availability on May 1, 2026 at $99/user/month, bundling E5 + Copilot + Agent 365 (monthly, annual, triennial terms via CSP). [source](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/security/blog/2026/05/01/microsoft-agent-365-now-generally-available-expands-capabilities-and-integrations/) — confidence: high
- **2026-05-11** — Agent 365 reached general availability on May 1, 2026 at $15/user/month as the central control plane for enterprise AI agents. - **2026-05-11** — Windows 365 Business license price reduction effective May 1, 2026 (immediate for new customers, on renewal for existing). [source](https://samexpert.com/microsoft-product-terms-may-2026/) — confidence: high
